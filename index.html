Anders Danielsen Lie

Actor, Musician and Medical Doctor.


Anders Danielsen Lie (born 1 January 1979) is a Norwegian actor, musician and medical doctor.He is the son of Amanda Award-winning actor Tone Danielsen. He met Norwegian model Iselin Steiro in 2007, and they married on 5 July 2008. They have two daughters together. His film debut was when he was 11 years old in the title role of Herman (1990) by Erik Gustavson.

Education

  • Ancient Greek (1997-1998) at the University of Oslo.
  • Musicology (2001-2003) at the University of Oslo.
  • Medicine (1999-2007) at the University of Oslo.
